How It Works

For Buyers

1
Create an Account

Sign up with your email and verify your account.

2
Verify Your Identity

Upload a valid government-issued ID for verification.

3
Add Payment Method

Add a credit card or bank account. A $0 authorization verifies your card.

4
Browse & Bid

Find vehicles you like and place bids or make offers.

5
Win & Pay

If you win, pay your invoice and we'll handle title transfer and transport.

For Sellers

1
Apply as a Seller

Create an account and apply for seller status.

2
Get Approved

Submit your dealer license (if applicable) for verification.

3
List Your Vehicle

Enter the VIN, upload photos, and set your pricing.

4
Auction Goes Live

Once approved, your vehicle is listed for buyers to bid on.

5
Get Paid

When the auction ends, receive payment and coordinate title transfer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Buyers pay a buyer's premium of 5% on the winning bid amount, plus applicable title and transport fees.

We partner with professional auto transport companies to deliver your vehicle. You'll receive a quote during checkout and can track your shipment in real-time.

We handle all title paperwork through our DMV integration. Once payment is complete, we process the title transfer and you'll receive your new title in the mail.
